Introduction & Historical Context

Figure P1: Historical timeline showing key discoveries in tetracycline research and staining identification

Discovery Timeline

  • 1948: Chlortetracycline discovered
  • 1950: Oxytetracycline isolated
  • 1953: Tetracycline developed
  • 1956: First discoloration reports
  • 1962: Definitive link established
  • 1970s: FDA black box warning

Key Researchers

  • Benjamin Duggar - Discovery
  • Schwachman et al. - Early warnings
  • Wallman & Hilton - Definitive proof
  • University of Liverpool - Longitudinal studies
